History Of Sweet Life

Menu

We offer a variety of delicious ice creams, sorbets, and toppings to satisfy any sweet tooth. Our three signature flavors of ice cream are

Sherbet

Sherbet is a frozen dessert made with fruit juice, sugar, and water, often with a dairy-free recipe.

Kulfi

Kulfi is a traditional Indian ice cream made with condensed milk and flavored with cardamom and saffron.

Snow Cream

Snow Cream is a Taiwanese style ice cream made with condensed milk.
